About Persist CONNECT

The neighborhood app is a platform that facilitates connections between neighbors by providing a virtual space where they can interact and stay updated on local activities. Similar to Facebook, this app allows users to engage with their neighbors, sharing information about ongoing events, gatherings, and community initiatives. It serves as a digital hub for residents to enhance their sense of community, fostering communication and collaboration within the neighborhood.
